Behavioral vs Structural Modelling

Behavioral

Describes the working of a system, without discussing its construction
arithmetic expression or truth tables used, gates used and thir nets not mentioned

It is the starting point, it is easier to write in Verilog


Structural

Dicusses Hardware implementation
Discusses gates and their interconnections


Behavorial Models are converted into Structrural description of system




In a Multiplexer,
Number of select lines is log_2(input lines)

    Use pure Behavorial
    Structural model using 4-to-1 multiplexer specufied using Behavorial
